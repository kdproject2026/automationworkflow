Land Acquisition SMS Outreach Sequence

Daily 1-week drip + long-term reactivation. GoHighLevel-ready merge fields.

Merge fields used: {{contact.first_name}} {{contact.address1}}
Maps directly to GHL standard contact fields. Keep messages short to avoid multi-segment billing. Always honor STOP replies immediately.

📩 Initial Outreach — Day 1

Send immediately

Goal: Spark curiosity, sound human, get a reply.

Template A — Direct & Friendly
Hey {{contact.first_name}}, we have caught interest in your lot at {{contact.address1}} are you ready to get rid of it?
Template B — Soft Question
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, are you the owner of the parcel at {{contact.address1}}? We're a local buyer interested in making a cash offer. Reply STOP to opt out.
Template C — Casual
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, hope you're well. We're interested in possibly buying your lot at {{contact.address1}}. Open to a quick chat about a cash offer? Reply STOP to opt out.

🔁 Follow-Up 1 — Day 2

+1 day, no reply

Goal: Bump to top of inbox without sounding pushy.

Template A
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, just circling back on your lot at {{contact.address1}}. Did our message reach you? Happy to share a cash offer if you're open to it.
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, no pressure at all — just making sure our note didn't get buried. Still interested in your parcel at {{contact.address1}} if you're considering selling.

🔁 Follow-Up 2 — Day 3

+2 days

Goal: Add value — speed, simplicity, no obligation.

Template A
Hey {{contact.first_name}}, we buy land directly — no agents, no closing costs, we cover everything. Still interested in your lot at {{contact.address1}} if you'd like a no-obligation number.
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, even if you're not ready to sell now, we can send a written offer for you to hold onto. No pressure. Want one for {{contact.address1}}?

🔁 Follow-Up 3 — Day 4

+3 days

Goal: Address common objections (taxes, holding costs).

Template A — Tax/Holding Angle
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, a lot of owners we work with hold land they no longer use but still pay taxes on it. If that sounds like {{contact.address1}}, we'd love to make it easy for you.
Template B — Price Curiosity
{{contact.first_name}}, totally understand if timing isn't right. Mind if we ask — is there a number that would make selling {{contact.address1}} a no-brainer for you?

🔁 Follow-Up 4 — Day 5

+4 days

Goal: Social proof + flexibility on timing.

Template A
Hey {{contact.first_name}}, we recently closed on a few parcels in your area. Quick, clean, no surprises. Open to seeing what we can offer for your lot at {{contact.address1}}?
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, we can work around your timeline — close in 2 weeks or 2 months, whatever works. Still interested if you'd like a fair cash offer on {{contact.address1}}.

🔁 Follow-Up 5 — Day 6

+5 days

Goal: Easy yes/no question to re-engage.

Template A
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, simple yes/no — are you open to selling {{contact.address1}} this year if the price was right?
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, would you rather we stop reaching out, or keep you on our list in case we can help later? Just want to respect your time.

🔁 Follow-Up 6 — Day 7

+6 days (closes week 1)

Goal: Polite closeout — leaves the door wide open.

Template A
{{contact.first_name}}, this'll be our last note for now. If selling {{contact.address1}} ever crosses your mind, text us anytime — we'll be ready. Thanks!
Template B
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, stepping back so we're not a bother. Our number stays the same — reach out whenever you're ready to talk about {{contact.address1}}. All the best.
⏸ WAIT 1 MONTH

📅 Reactivation 1 — 1 Month Later

+30 days from Day 7

Goal: Light touch, fresh angle.

Template A
Hey {{contact.first_name}}, checking in one more time on {{contact.address1}}. Anything change on your end? Still happy to make an offer if it's useful.
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, market's been active in your area lately. Want us to pull a fresh number on your lot at {{contact.address1}}?

📅 Reactivation 2 — 1 Year Later

+12 months (final touch)

Goal: Annual check-in — life changes (taxes, inheritance, retirement).

Template A
Hey {{contact.first_name}}, it's been about a year since we last connected about {{contact.address1}}. Things change — figured we'd check if selling is on your radar now.
Template B
{{contact.first_name}}, annual check-in 👋 Still own the lot at {{contact.address1}}? If you're ready to offload it, we're ready to buy. Cash, fast close.
Template C — Final Closer
Hi {{contact.first_name}}, last check-in from us on {{contact.address1}}. If anything ever changes, you've got our number. Wishing you the best.
